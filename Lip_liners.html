Lip Liners

Result

Boost your lip game by using a lip liner from SkinStore. Lip liners are makeup bag essentials that help to create the illusion of fuller, more youthful, and defined lips. Paired with the perfect lipstick, a lip pencil can prevent smudging and bleeding by providing a product for lipstick to adhere to, consequently increasing its staying power. It can also be used instead of lipstick, giving the lips a matte finish and making them look completely gorgeous. With a range of colors from subdued nudes like the Kevyn Aucoin Unforgettable Lip Definer to sultry reds (Elizabeth Arden Beautiful Color Precision Glide Lipliner), SkinStore has a liner for every occasion.
